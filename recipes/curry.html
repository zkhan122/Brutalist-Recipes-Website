Sora X Food: Curry Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 50 ml Vegetable Oil
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 Onions finely diced
  • 3 Tomatoes chopped
  • 10 Garlic Cloves minced
  • 1 inch Ginger crushed
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Dried Fenugreek methi
  • 2 Green Chillies
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Purée
  • 500 ml Water warm
  • 1 tbsp Cumin Seeds crushed
  • Coriander
      • Instructions
    1. In a large pot heat up the vegetable oil on medium heat until hot
    2. Add the finely diced onions, garlic cloves, crushed ginger and salt then cook for 3-4 minutes until the onions are slightly softened –
    3. adding the salt with the onions helps to bring the moisture out of the onion to allow them to cook down and brown more evenly without burning the onions
    4. Add the chopped tomatoes then cook for 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes soften
    5. Add the chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powderand the dried fenugreek leaves
    6. then cook the spices for 4-6 minutes adding a few tablespoons of water to prevent the spices from burning
    7. Add the finely chopped green chilies, coriander and tomato purée and cook for another 1-2 minutes
    8. Add the chicken into the pan and gently mix the spices into the chicken before covering
    9. with a lid and cooking for 15-20 minutes until the chicken has almost cooked through
    10. Add the warm water and allow it to come to a gentle simmer then cook for 6-8 minutes or until thickened to your personal preference
    11. Add the crushed cumin seeds then cook for 1 minute until the oil has separated –
    12. the oil has separated when there are small bubbles appearing and the oil has formed a thin layer on top
      13. Your chicken curry is ready!
