Sora X Food

Ingredients:
6 portions ramen noodles, fresh or instant
Soy-marinated eggs
Marinated bamboo shoots
Chopped green onion
For the soy sauce broth:
3 l water
900 g chicken bones/chicken breast (meat)
3 cloves garlic, peeled
1 piece ginger, sliced
3 green onions, roughly chopped
250 ml soy sauce
For the chashu sauce:
160 ml water
80 ml soy sauce
3 tbsp sugar
Method:
  1. Rinse the chicken bones in water, then place them in a large pot with the water, garlic, ginger, and green onions.
  2. Bring to the boil, removing the dirt that comes to the surface. Lower the heat and simmer with the pot covered for around two hours.
  3. Then strain off the broth, getting rid of the other ingredients.
  4. Add the soy sauce, then leave to simmer with the pot covered until ready to serve.
  5. Mix the ingredients for the chashu sauce in a large pan. Add the meat, then bring to the boil over a medium heat.
  6. Cover with a lid, leaving slightly open so the steam can escape, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for around 30 minutes,
    7. turning the meat from time to time until just a little bit of liquid is left.
  7. Remove the lid, stay with the pan, and watch the liquid simmer until bubbles start to appear.
  8. Turn the meat to cover it completely with the sauce, then remove from the pan and slice thinly.
  9. When ready to serve, cook the ramen flilowing the instructions on the packaging.
  10. Divide the broth into six bowls, then add the noodles, meat, bamboo shoots, soy-marinated eggs, and green onions.
  11. Serve hot.
